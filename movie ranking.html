The Best Movies
According to Kanha

My top 3 movies of all-time:

  • Inception

    This movie's direction, combined with the great acting and a unique theme, make Inception timeless.

  • The Matrix

    The idea of us people living inside a fake world, along with the character development, made this movie great.

  • Interstellar

    The narration and theme of this movie is truly remarkable. Interstellar has to be one of the best space movies of all time.
